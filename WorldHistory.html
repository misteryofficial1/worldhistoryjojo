About

The purpose of this website is to show people the truth behind the history of all countries, continents, and persons whom made an impact on global society.

I made this website with the intent of publishing and releasing real historical information to the public. This is especially important to people from countries, where their freedom of speech and press are stripped of by the governments. People might think that their countries' origins started off with prosperity and stayed that way throughout, but that wasn't the case. To hide the dark truth behind the events that lead to the establishments of their respective nations and what transpired after that. Manipulation is becoming a problem of the present, and possibly the future if we were to be so ignorant and vulnerable.
