Key Concepts of World History

Full List

Imperialism
A policy of extending a country's power and influence through colonization, use of military force, or other means.
Nationalism
A feeling of loyalty and devotion to one's country, often accompanied by a desire for independence or self-governance.
Colonialism
The practice of one country establishing and maintaining colonies in other territories, often for economic or strategic gain.
